Max The Monkey

Though Max the Monkey attempts excelled more than others, him nor his apprentice Nugget have the capability to stop what happens to them.

Personality

Cheerful and playful, Max often found himself in disturbing predicaments due to his lack of intelligence. He is friendly and well-meaning, but a terrible character due to his flaws.

Death

Max's death was caused by his low intelligence, he had been brutally killed and stretched by someone unknown. He bled out on the floor before having his right arm and neck being stretched out, his arm being dislocated. He was then dragged away by his legs and thrown into a dumpster.
